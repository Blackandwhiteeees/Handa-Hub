About Emergency Hotlines & Resources

Preparedness is not just about knowing what to do — it’s about trusting credible, Filipino-based sources. HANDA Hub connects you to official agencies that provide real-time updates, safety tools, and verified information you can rely on during disasters.

Why These Resources Matter

During emergencies, accurate information can save lives. These agencies provide essential warnings, hotline assistance, rescue operations, and relief services. Keeping these contacts accessible helps you act quickly and responsibly in times of crisis.
